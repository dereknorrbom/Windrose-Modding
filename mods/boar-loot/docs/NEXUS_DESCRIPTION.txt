[size=5][b]Boar Loot x3 (Meat + Hide)[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Increases boar leather and meat drops by 3x while keeping drop logic and rarity structure intact.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Description[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]This mod multiplies boar leather and boar meat quantities by 3x across standard, F, and Mega boar loot tables. It is designed for players who want better hunting rewards without changing unrelated systems.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]What It Changes[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Boar leather drop quantities are multiplied by 3x
[*]Boar meat drop quantities are multiplied by 3x
[*]Zero-value entries remain zero (no forced guaranteed drops)
[*]No edits to non-boar loot tables
[/list]

[size=4][b]Install[/b][/size]
[list=1]
[*]Extract the mod archive
[*]Copy [color=#D4D4D8]BoarLoot_P.pak[/color] into [color=#D4D4D8]Windrose\R5\Content\Paks\~mods[/color]
[*]Launch the game
[/list]

[size=4][b]Uninstall[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Remove [color=#D4D4D8]BoarLoot_P.pak[/color] from [color=#D4D4D8]Windrose\R5\Content\Paks\~mods[/color].[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Compatibility[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Incompatible with other mods that override the same boar loot table assets. Compatible with most mods that do not touch boar RSS loot tables.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Version[/b][/size]
