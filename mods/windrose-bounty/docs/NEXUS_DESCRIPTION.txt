[size=5][b]Windrose Bounty - Loot Variants (2x / 3x / 5x / 10x)[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]A growing all-in-one bounty bundle for players who want the resource boosts without juggling a pile of separate pak files.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]What This Mod Does[/b][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]Scales Min/Max quantities for boar loot, cayenne peppers, crab loot, crocodile loot, goat loot, sweet potatoes, and wolf loot where those drops exist. Covers Boar Loot, Cayenne Bounty, Crab Bounty, Crocodile Bounty, Goat Bounty, Sweet Potato Bounty, and Wolf Bounty. Drop chances and weights are left unchanged.[/color][/size]
[size=3][color=#D4D4D8]I am still learning Windrose modding as I go, and this bundle grew out of playing the game, running into new resource bottlenecks, and getting helpful requests from other players. As I keep exploring new areas and finding new types of loot, Windrose Bounty will likely grow with it. The individual mods remain available for players who only want specific resources boosted.[/color][/size]

[size=4][b]Available Variants[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][b]2x[/b] - Light boost
[*][b]3x[/b] - Balanced default
[*][b]5x[/b] - Faster progression
[*][b]10x[/b] - High abundance / low grind
[/list]

[size=4][b]Important Notes[/b][/size]
[list]
[*]Drop chance logic is unchanged from vanilla
[*]If an entry is 0/0 in vanilla, it remains 0/0
[*]Only quantity values are multiplied
[*]Use only one variant `.pak` at a time
[/list]

[size=4][b]Install[/b][/size]
[list=1]
[*]Choose one variant `.pak`
[*]Install it to the correct folder for how you are playing
[*]Launch game
[/list]

[list]
[*][b]Single-player:[/b] [color=#D4D4D8]Windrose\R5\Content\Paks\~mods[/color]
